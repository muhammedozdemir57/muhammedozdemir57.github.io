Se7en is a 1995 American psychological thriller film directed by David Fincher and written by Andrew Kevin Walker. It stars Brad Pitt, Morgan Freeman, Gwyneth Paltrow, and Kevin Spacey. The film tells the story of two detectives, a rookie and a veteran, who hunt a serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as his modus operandi.

The film follows the detectives as they become entwined in the case. The movie has become a cult classic and has been praised for its intriguing plot, incredible performances, and intense atmosphere. The film has been credited with popularizing the idea of using the seven deadly sins as a basis for a serial killer's motives.

The movie explores the concept of the seven deadly sins and how they can manifest themselves in the real world. It delves into the idea of justice, morality, and the consequences of our actions. The film also examines the idea of redemption and how far people are willing to go to seek it.

Overall, Se7en is a thrilling and thought-provoking film that has withstood the test of time and remains one of the best psychological thrillers of all time. It is a must-watch for anyone looking for an intense and thought-provoking movie experience.
